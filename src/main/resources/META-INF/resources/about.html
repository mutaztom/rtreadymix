Who We Are:

Knowledge & Training Center is one of the pioneer Training Centers in Sudan and North-East Africa, founded in 2019. KTC has developed its training potential and geared up to meet the training needs of various industries with emphasis on the Oil, Gas and Mineral industry.

Our mission is to develop goal-focused, human competencies and skills and enable our students to build capacities through learning and knowledge sharing

What we do:

At KTC the training rooms are fully equipped with the needed tools, and the learning environment is conducive. We are committed to providing the ultimate knowledge and training experience to our clients, students, and community as a whole. By entering into strategic partnerships we intend to complement, not compete; KTC’s market share measures are based on balancing and harmonizing knowledge and skill.

Our vision is to be the most goal-oriented knowledge and training center, focusing on ourclient’s success and helping organizations in achieving their goals.

Why Choose KTC

The team of KTC is dedicated and committed towards excellence and their tremendous experience in the specialized training disciplines set the basic cornerstone for the center.

Besides classroom and hands-on-training, KTC provides comprehensive consultancy services in the Oil and Gas industry, Geosciences and Engineering, Mineral Resources,Human Capital, and Education including full-service e-Learning and Learning Management System consultation.
